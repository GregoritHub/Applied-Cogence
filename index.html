7-Year Arc Overview

Compound layers: cycle each quadrant once per year (I / We / It / Its), then repeat at higher resolution. Deliverables, metrics, and failure controls are hard-wired. No drift.

Cadence (Non-Negotiable)

  • Weekly: 5×90min reading; 3×120min making; 1 ethics drill; daily dream log; 1 analysis session
  • Monthly: 1 public artifact; 1 external critique; 1 dialogue/mentoring hour
  • Quarterly: Capstone + 2-page retrospective

Metrics

Track I/We/It/Its indicators as specified. Progress is persisted in your browser (localStorage).

Failure Modes → Controls

  • Enantiodromia: if any quadrant >45% for 3 weeks, auto-alert to rebalance next week
  • Inflation: supervision required for practice; projection logs; amplify before advice
  • Performativity: require written conscience-based justification
  • Aesthetic fetishism: reject work if comprehension <70% regardless of beauty
  • Systems drift: quarterly naïve-user red-team

Reading / Budget Gatekeeping

Free first. Paid only after 6-week plateau or concrete question demands depth. Ordered lists per quadrant embedded in task details.

Proof of Learning

  • Y1: 12 artifacts + 12 summaries + 6 case briefs … → Y7: Methods handbook + 4 recorded lectures

Self-Calibration Prompts

  • I: “What compensates my one-sidedness this month…?”
  • We: “Which virtue actually moved a decision…?”
  • It: “What proportion decision improved comprehension…?”
  • Its: “Where did feedback reduce uncertainty…?”
